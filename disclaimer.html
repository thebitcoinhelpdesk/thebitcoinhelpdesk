Bitcoin Security

Bitcoin ownership is determined by ownership of private keys. Anyone in the world with access to private keys has full control over the corresponding Bitcoin.

  • NEVER share your private keys with anyone unless you trust them will full control over your Bitcoin
  • NEVER enter your private keys into a computer
  • NEVER expose your keys in front of a camera
  • NEVER speak your private keys out loud especially around others or microphones

We will NEVER ask for the private keys to your Bitcoin and will NEVER need access to your private keys to provide consulting. Sensitive operations involving private keys will be handled securely by you out of view of cameras and typically on dedicated secure hardware devices detached from your computer and the internet. You remain in full control of your Bitcoin at all times.

If your private keys are ever exposed inadvertently during a consulting session, We will guide you through creating a new wallet with new private keys and transferring your Bitcoin to that new wallet.

Bitcoin Volatility

In the short term, Bitcoin price action can be highly volatile and unpredictable. Over the long term, Bitcoin has a proven 15-year record of being one of the best performing asset classes. Our guidance is best suited for low time preference individuals looking to build a nest egg for their future.
