About

Mike is a passionate Bitcoiner with 25 years of experience in system administration, computer science, and technology and more recent experience studying Austrian economics, finance, libertarianism, and the latest trends and best practices in Bitcoin.

Mike can help strengthen your understanding and conviction about Bitcoin and the future and then guide you through your Bitcoin journey, from acquiring Bitcoin, to taking it into self-custody and securing it properly, to improving privacy and security by running your own node, to building an inheritance plan and leaving behind a financial legacy for your family.

Whether you're new to Bitcoin and starting from ground zero or have a solid foundation already and would appreciate a mentor that can accelerate your progression to Bitcoin mastery, Mike can help you meet your goals.
